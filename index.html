About Sling

Born in a tiny backyard with a borrowed oven, Sling now slings hundreds of pies a day across the city. We source local, knead slow‑fermented dough, and fire at blistering heat for that crisp‑chewy bite.

  • 48‑hour cold‑fermented dough
  • Hand‑crushed San Marzano tomatoes
  • Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten‑free options
  • Eco‑friendly packaging
